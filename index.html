About Friends

Friends is a popular American sitcom that aired from 1994 to 2004. It revolves around a group of six friends living in Manhattan, New York City, as they navigate through the ups and downs of life, love, and careers.

The main characters include Rachel, Ross, Monica, Chandler, Joey, and Phoebe, each with their own distinct personalities and quirks.
